My research interests primarily lie in sociolinguistics, sound, and semiotics. More specifically, I am interested in how phonetic variation works in tandem with other resources to index social meaning.

I use a variety of quantitative and qualitative methods, ranging from experiments to ethnography, to investigate these questions.
