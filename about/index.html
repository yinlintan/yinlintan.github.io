I'm currently interning at RICE Media and KrASIA. I've also freelanced for RADII China, What's On Weibo, and Atlas Obscura. In a previous life, I was a linguist with a specific interest in all aspects of bilingualism.

The world is really cool and I do my best to understand it through journalism, writing about stuff that inspires curiosity and change.

I primarily cover tech, culture, and current affairs. I'm particularly interested in subcultures, social media, the intersection of culture and tech, and the arts. Most of my articles are about Singapore, where I'm currently based in, but I write about anything related to China and Southeast Asia in general.

Some other career-related things I do, other than writing/reporting/editing: photography, illustration, graphic design, web design, and English-Mandarin translation.

I am fluent in English and Mandarin, used to learn French, and am learning Japanese and Korean.

When not writing, I can be found at dance sessions/trainings, practising ollies, running, planning my next trip, furiously editing this website, or dabbling in other creative pursuits.
